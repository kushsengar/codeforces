LifeLane booking FAQs

What is the toll-free emergency ambulance number in india?

Dial 108 for Emergency Medical Response Ambulance Service, 102 for the ambulance service for pregnant women and infants. For private ambulance service, dial: 18008-908-208 & call an ambulance now!

Is pre-booking available with LifeLane?

Yes,LifeLane provides pre-booking options through our toll-free number at affordable rates. You can book an ambulance in advance to ensure availability in case of emergencies.

Can I choose the type of ambulance I need?

Yes, you can choose the type of ambulance based on the emergency. We offer basic life support (BLS), advanced life support (ALS), ICU ambulances. Our team will also help you choose the right ambulance based on the patient's condition.

What payment modes do you accept?

LifeLane accepts multiple payment modes for booking an ambulance such as cash, credit/debit cards and online wallets. You can also choose to pay through our app or website using a secure payment gateway.
